Airbnb Data Analysis Graphs

Graph 1: Average Price by City

This graph shows the average Airbnb price across different cities. Cities with higher tourism demand tend to have higher prices.

Graph 2: Price vs Rating

This graph compares listing prices with overall ratings. It helps us see whether higher-rated places cost more.

Graph 3: Price vs Latitude

This graph shows how Airbnb prices vary by location (latitude). There is no strong pattern, meaning location alone does not determine price.
